Mechanics
How it actually works

Velomon connects to your real rides. Every kilometer and every meter of height translates directly into in-game power — no grinding, no farming, just cycling.

01
Connect your GPS or Strava
Import past rides or track new ones in real time. Indoor trainers and smart watches work too. No special hardware required — your phone GPS is enough.
Strava · Garmin · Wahoo · Apple Health
02
Flat roads give XP, climbs give Gold
Every kilometer earns your pet experience points. Every meter of elevation gain earns you Gold — the premium in-game currency. Sprinters and climbers are rewarded differently.
1 km = 10 XP · 10 m altitude = 1 Gold
03
Your pet levels up and evolves
XP fills your level bar. At certain milestones your pet evolves — changing appearance, unlocking new abilities, and growing battle stats. There are 4 evolution stages per pet.
4 evolution stages · Unique abilities
04
Battle trainers online
Challenge other riders in real-time PvP battles. Your pet's stats mirror your training effort — no pay-to-win advantage. Fight in regional leagues or global ranked matches.
Global ranked · Regional cups
05
Compete in seasons and events
Every 3 months a new season resets global rankings and introduces new pets. Regional events tie the game to real cycling routes near you — finish them for exclusive rewards.
Seasonal resets · Local routes · Exclusive pets

FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a smart bike or special hardware?
No. Your phone GPS is enough to log rides. You can also connect Strava, Garmin, Wahoo, Apple Health, or any GPX-compatible app. Indoor trainers with Bluetooth also work.
Can someone with more money have a stronger pet?
No. Pet stats are calculated exclusively from your ride data — kilometers and elevation. There are no premium power boosts. Gold (earned from climbs) only buys cosmetics and items, never stat increases.
What happens to my pet between seasons?
Your pet's level and evolution stay permanently. Only leaderboard rankings reset at the start of each season. You keep your pet forever — it just starts a new season in a fresh competitive pool.
Can I have more than one pet?
Every player starts with exactly one pet. Additional pets can be earned (not bought) through event completions, season rewards, and special milestones. Each additional pet slot is earned through riding.
How do battles work exactly?
Battles are turn-based, using your pet's ATK, DEF, SPD, and STA stats. SPD determines who goes first. Each pet has 3 learnable abilities unlocked through leveling. Battles last a maximum of 10 rounds.
What counts as an indoor ride?
Turbo trainers and smart bikes connected via Bluetooth or ANT+ are fully supported. Indoor rides earn XP normally. Elevation data from virtual climbs (Zwift, etc.) counts for Gold at 50% of the outdoor rate.
Are there regional events outside of Germany?
Yes. We currently run events in 6 regions — Germany, Austria, Switzerland, France, Netherlands, and the UK. More regions are added each season based on rider demand. Vote at velomon.io/regions.
Is the game free to play?
Completely free to start and to compete. There are optional cosmetic purchases (skins, accessories, profile themes) but nothing that affects gameplay performance. All event rewards are earnable for free.
